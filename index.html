About the Trust

A registered charitable trust dedicated to pilgrim seva and community uplift.

Sri Vasavi Amma Seva Trust is dedicated to serving pilgrims and society through annadanam, education, medical care, orphan support, pilgrim accommodation, temple infrastructure, and youth development.

We believe that every act of service uplifts humanity and earns divine blessings. Operating from Proddatur in Kadapa District, Andhra Pradesh, every rupee is accounted for and directed to active seva work at major temple centres across the state.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about donations, 80G, membership and our seva work.

Have a different question? Call 9052963152 or write to svamsevatrust@gmail.com.

What is Sri Vasavi Amma Seva Trust?

We are a registered charitable trust based in Proddatur, Andhra Pradesh. We serve pilgrims and communities through annadanam (free meals), education support, medical aid, orphan care, and pilgrim accommodation at major temple centres including Tirumala, Arunachalam, and Penugonda.

Is my donation eligible for 80G tax exemption?

Yes. Donations are eligible for 80G tax exemption under URN ABOTS1850PF2026101, effective 30 March 2026, valid for assessment years 2026–27 through 2028–29. Please retain your donation receipt for income tax filing.

How can I donate?

You can donate via bank transfer to our HDFC Bank account (A/c 50200120468025, IFSC HDFC0006514) or call 9052963152 for guidance. Full bank details are listed in the Donate section above.

What is donor membership and what are the benefits?

Donor membership is available at ₹50,000 for up to four members. Members receive 3 days of complimentary accommodation at our pilgrim facilities. Contact us at 9052963152 or svamsevatrust@gmail.com to enquire.

Where does the trust provide pilgrim accommodation?

We support pilgrims visiting Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple (Tirumala), Sri Arunachaleswara Swamy Temple (Arunachalam), and Sri Penugonda Ammavari Temple (Penugonda). All seva activities are coordinated from Proddatur, Kadapa District.

What seva activities does the trust perform?

Our seva includes annadanam (free meals), education support for students, medical assistance, care for orphans, pilgrim lodging, construction of Kalyana Mandapams at pilgrimage centres, and special programs supporting athletes, IT professionals, and youth employment.
